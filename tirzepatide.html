Product Description

Tirzepatide is a novel dual glucose-dependent insulinotropic polypeptide (GIP) and glucagon-like peptide-1 (GLP-1) receptor agonist. It represents a new class of incretin-based therapies that simultaneously targets two distinct metabolic hormone receptors.

As a synthetic peptide analog, Tirzepatide mimics the actions of natural incretin hormones, which play crucial roles in glucose homeostasis and metabolic regulation. The dual agonist mechanism offers enhanced metabolic benefits compared to single-receptor agonists.

Key Features:

  • Dual receptor targeting (GLP-1 and GIP receptors)
  • Enhanced insulin secretion in glucose-dependent manner
  • Prolonged half-life suitable for research applications
  • High purity ≥99% with comprehensive QC documentation
  • Available in multiple packaging options

Research Applications
