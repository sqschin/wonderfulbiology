Product Description

GHK-Cu (Glycyl-L-Histidyl-L-Lysine Copper) is a naturally occurring copper complex of the tripeptide glycyl-histidyl-lysine. First isolated from human plasma, this remarkable peptide has gained significant attention in cosmetic and dermatological research for its skin regeneration properties.

The copper ion in GHK-Cu plays a crucial role in its biological activity, facilitating cellular processes involved in tissue repair and remodeling. With its small molecular size, GHK-Cu can effectively penetrate the skin barrier, making it valuable for topical formulation research.

Key Features:

  • Natural tripeptide with copper ion complex
  • Small molecular weight for enhanced penetration
  • High stability in cosmetic formulations
  • Water-soluble for easy formulation integration
  • Cosmetic-grade purity ≥99%

Research Applications

GHK-Cu is extensively studied in cosmetic and dermatological research:

  • Wound Healing: Tissue repair and regeneration mechanisms
