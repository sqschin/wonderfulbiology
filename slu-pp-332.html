What is SLU-PP-332?

SLU-PP-332 (CAS 303760-60-3) is a synthetic compound that acts as a pan-agonist for the Estrogen-Related Receptor (ERR) family, particularly ERRα, ERRβ, and ERRγ. These nuclear receptors play crucial roles in regulating energy metabolism, mitochondrial biogenesis, and oxidative metabolism in tissues with high energy demands.

Research has shown that SLU-PP-332 can mimic some of the metabolic effects of physical exercise, leading to its characterization as an "exercise mimetic." Studies in animal models have demonstrated improvements in endurance capacity, fat oxidation, and metabolic health markers.
