Product Description

Argireline (Acetyl Hexapeptide-8) is a synthetic peptide specifically designed for cosmetic applications. This six-amino acid peptide has gained significant attention in the skincare industry for its potential to reduce the appearance of expression lines and wrinkles.

As a water-soluble peptide, Argireline can be easily incorporated into various cosmetic formulations including serums, creams, and lotions. Its small molecular size allows for effective penetration when applied topically, making it valuable for cosmetic formulation research.

Key Features:

  • Synthetic hexapeptide for cosmetic research
  • Water-soluble for easy formulation integration
  • Small molecular weight for enhanced skin penetration
  • Stable in typical cosmetic pH ranges
